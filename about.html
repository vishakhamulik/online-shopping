About Us

           Welcome to VIshop, your number one source for all things like grocery,Vegetables,fruits,chocolates etc. We're dedicated to giving you the very best and fresh vegetavles and fruits, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 1990 by Vishakha Mulik, VIshop has come a long way from its beginnings in a Anpatwadi near satara. When first started out, her passion for providing fresh and afortable grocery product to customer providing the best products drove him to quit her day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Maharastra, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the online grocery industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, VIshop Vishakha Mulik CEO, Founder
